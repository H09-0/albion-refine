Guides & Strategy

Learn the mechanics and optimize your refining.

Getting Started

Refining converts raw gathered resources into refined materials used for crafting. There are 5 disciplines: Wood→Planks, Ore→Bars, Fiber→Cloth, Hide→Leather, Stone→Blocks.

Each royal city gives a +40% refining bonus to one specific resource type. Always refine in the correct bonus city for max returns.

The City Bonus Paradox

Because everyone refines in the bonus city, raw materials are more expensive and refined materials are cheaper there due to competition.

Better strategy: Buy raw in a non-bonus city, transport to the bonus city to refine, then sell the refined materials elsewhere (or Black Market).

Focus Optimization

Focus adds +59% production bonus. With max spec (40k efficiency), focus cost drops to 6.25% of base.

Players report ~145 SPF for T6.3 refining at high spec = ~1.45M silver/day from 10k Focus.

Spec Leveling

Start with T4, work up to T8. Each 10k focus cost efficiency halves your focus cost. Max all refining nodes for 40k efficiency.

Higher tiers consume more raw per refined unit (T4: 3→2, T8: 5→2), making RRR increasingly important.
