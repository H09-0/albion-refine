# Refining in Albion Online

Refining is the process of converting raw gathered resources into refined materials used for crafting equipment, weapons, tools, and consumables.

## Why Refine?

- **Profit**: Buy raw resources from the market, refine them, sell the refined materials for a margin
- **Self-Sufficiency**: Supply your own crafting operations with refined materials
- **Fame**: Gain crafting fame and level up refining specializations on the Destiny Board
- **Focus Efficiency**: Premium players can use Focus to increase Resource Return Rate (RRR) and maximize profits

## The 5 Refining Disciplines

| Raw Resource | Refined Material | Refining Building | Bonus City |
|-------------|-----------------|-------------------|------------|
| Wood        | Planks          | Lumbermill        | Fort Sterling |
| Ore         | Metal Bars      | Smelter           | Thetford |
| Fiber       | Cloth           | Weaver            | Lymhurst |
| Hide        | Leather         | Tanner            | Martlock |
| Stone       | Stone Blocks    | Stonemason        | Bridgewatch |

## Refining Stations

Refining takes place at player-built stations located in:
- **Royal Cities** (open to all, but usage fees apply)
- **Islands** (personal or guild, no usage fee but no city bonuses)
- **Hideouts** in the Outlands (no usage fee, can have station bonuses)
- **Caerleon** and **Brecilien** (neutral cities with different bonus profiles)

## Key Concepts

- **Resource Return Rate (RRR)**: % of materials returned after refining
- **Focus**: Premium resource that boosts RRR by ~59% when used
- **City Bonus**: Each royal city has a +40% refining bonus for one specific resource type
- **Daily Bonus**: Two randomly selected items get +10% or +20% production bonus each day
- **Specialization**: Leveling refining nodes reduces Focus cost and improves efficiency
