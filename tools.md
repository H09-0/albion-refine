# External Tools & Calculators

## Web-Based Calculators

| Tool | URL | Features |
|------|-----|----------|
| **Albion Free Market** | https://albionfreemarket.com/crafting | Full crafting/refining calc with live prices, specs sync, shopping lists |
| **ReFineStation** | https://refinestation.com | Dedicated refining calculator with focus optimization |
| **Albion Profit Calculator** | https://albion-profit-calculator.com | Multi-tool: refining, transmuting, food, potions |
| **OneLifeGaming** | https://onelifegaming.com/albion-online | Market tracker, crafting calc, price alerts, gold market |
| **Albion Online Builds** | https://www.albiononlinebuilds.com/tools/craft-calculator | Craft profit calc with live market data |
| **AlbionKit** | https://albionkit.com/profits/crafting | Crafting calculator + market flipper |
| **Albion Database** | https://www.albiondatabase.com/tools/crafting-calculator | Simple crafting profit calculator |

## Data Sources

- **Albion Online Data Project (AODP)**: https://www.albion-online-data.com/ — API providing real-time market prices used by most calculators
- **Albion Online Wiki**: https://wiki.albiononline.com — Official game wiki with detailed mechanics

## Community Resources

- **r/albiononline**: Reddit community with guides and discussions
- **Albion Online Forum**: https://forum.albiononline.com — Official forums
- **Discord**: Various trading/crafting community Discords

## Price Checking

Most calculators pull real-time prices from AODP. For manual checking:
- Open the in-game market in each city
- Check https://albionfreemarket.com for cross-city price comparisons
