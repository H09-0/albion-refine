# Optimal Refining Strategies

## 1. Transport Refining (Recommended)

> **Key community insight**: Because everyone refines in the bonus city, raw materials are typically MORE expensive there and refined materials are CHEAPER. The real profit comes from buying raw in non-bonus cities, transporting to the bonus city, refining, then transporting the refined materials to sell where they're priced higher.

1. Buy raw resources from a non-bonus city (usually cheaper there)
2. Transport them to the bonus city for that resource
3. Refine with Focus and daily bonus for max RRR
4. Transport refined materials to other cities (or Caerleon/Black Market) to sell

**Example**: Buy Ore in Martlock (cheaper), transport to Thetford (ore bonus), refine with Focus, sell Metal Bars in Fort Sterling or Black Market.

**Best for**: Players willing to transport; significantly better margins

## 2. City Bonus Refining (Simpler, Lower Effort)

1. Choose one resource to specialize in
2. Refine in its bonus city (e.g., Wood → Fort Sterling)
3. Buy raw materials using buy orders (even at bonus city prices)
4. Refine with Focus during daily bonus days
5. Sell refined materials with sell orders

**Best for**: Stable, predictable profit with minimal transport. Lower margins than transport refining, but less effort.

## 3. Hideout Refining (Advanced)

1. Join/start a guild with a powered-up hideout
2. Gather or buy raw materials in the black zone
3. Refine at the hideout (no city taxes)
4. Transport refined goods to royal cities for sale

**Best for**: Black zone players, large-scale operations

## 4. Focus Allocation Strategy

With 10,000 Focus daily:
- Prioritize the highest Silver Per Focus (SPF) crafts
- T6-T8 refining typically gives the best SPF
- Higher enchantments (.1, .2, .3) often give better SPF than .0
- **Real community numbers**: Players report ~145 SPF for T6.3 ore refining in Thetford, netting ~1.45M silver per day from 10k Focus
- Use all available Focus across multiple characters if you have alts
- At max spec (6.25% focus cost), your daily Focus goes much further

## 5. Spec Leveling Strategy

1. Start with T4 refining (lowest barrier to entry)
2. As you gain specs, move to higher tiers
3. Focus on one refining type until it's maxed
4. Max spec = 40,000 Focus Cost Efficiency (6.25% of base cost)

## 6. Daily Bonus Optimization

- Check which items have the daily bonus
- If your chosen resource has the bonus, refine as much as possible that day
- If not, consider refining a different resource that does have the bonus
- Stack with Focus for maximum RRR

## 7. Economics Tips

- **Buy low, sell high**: Use buy orders for raw, sell orders for refined
- **Watch station fees**: High-population cities often have higher refining taxes
- **Volume matters**: Thin margins on refining mean you need scale
- **Watch the market**: Prices fluctuate based on player activity, patches, and events
- **Journal profit**: Using journals adds extra profit — always use them if the math works
- **The paradox of city bonuses**: The bonus city has inflated raw prices and depressed refined prices due to high competition. The real margin is in buying raw elsewhere and transporting.
