## Enchantment Levels

| Enchantment | Name | Material Multiplier |
|-------------|------|-------------------|
| .0 | Common | 1x |
| .1 | Uncommon | ~1.75x |
| .2 | Rare | ~3x |
| .3 | Exceptional | ~5.4x |
| .4 | Pristine | ~9.4x |

Higher enchantments require more raw materials per unit and cost more Focus, but the refined product sells for significantly more.

## Refining Ratios

Typically, refining follows a roughly **2:1** ratio of raw to refined:
- 2 raw resources → 1 refined material (varies by tier)

## Specialization Tree

To unlock higher-tier refining, you must craft lower-tier refined materials:

| Node Name | Tier Unlocked |
|-----------|--------------|
| Journeyman Refiner | Prerequisite |
| Adept [Resource] Refiner | T4 |
| Expert [Resource] Refiner | T5 |
| Master [Resource] Refiner | T6 |
| Grandmaster [Resource] Refiner | T7 |
| Elder [Resource] Refiner | T8 |
