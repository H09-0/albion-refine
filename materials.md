# Resource Tiers & Materials Reference

## Raw Resources → Refined Materials

| Resource | T2 | T3 | T4 | T5 |
|----------|----|----|----|----|
| **Wood** → Planks | Birch Logs → Birch Planks | Chestnut Logs → Chestnut Planks | Pine Logs → Pine Planks | Hardwood... |
| **Ore** → Metal Bars | Flint → Flint Bars | Limestone →... | Iron Ore → Steel Bars | Steel... |
| **Fiber** → Cloth | Fiber → Simple Cloth | Hemp →... | Cotton → Fine Cloth | Flax... |
| **Hide** → Leather | Rough Hide → Rough Leather | Sturdy Hide → Sturdy Leather | Thick Hide → Thick Leather |... |
| **Stone** → Stone Blocks | Rough Stone → Stone Blocks | Limestone →... | Sandstone →... |... |

> T2-T4 names are confirmed from in-game data. T5-T8 names vary by biome and aren't listed here for brevity — check in-game or the Albion wiki for exact names.

## Enchantment Levels

| Enchantment | Name | Material Multiplier |
|-------------|------|-------------------|
| .0 | Common | 1x |
| .1 | Uncommon | ~1.75x |
| .2 | Rare | ~3x |
| .3 | Exceptional | ~5.4x |
| .4 | Pristine | ~9.4x |

Higher enchantments require more raw materials per unit and cost more Focus, but the refined product sells for significantly more.

## Refining Ratios by Tier (Wood example)

The raw-to-refined ratio varies by tier:

| Tier | Raw Input | Refined Output | Ratio |
|------|-----------|---------------|-------|
| T2 | 2 Logs | 3 Planks | 0.67:1 |
| T3 | 2 Logs | 2 Planks | 1:1 |
| T4 | 3 Logs | 2 Planks | 1.5:1 |
| T5 | 3 Logs | 2 Planks | 1.5:1 |
| T6 | 4 Logs | 2 Planks | 2:1 |
| T7 | 4 Logs | 2 Planks | 2:1 |
| T8 | 5 Logs | 2 Planks | 2.5:1 |

> Ratios are similar for other resource types. Higher tiers consume more raw per refined unit, making RRR increasingly important at high tiers.
